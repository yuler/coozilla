What is Coozilla?

CooZilla helps you hire Web freelancers in China. If you post a job on CooZilla , and the most relevant freelancers will be instantly informed by email and you will receive a periodic notification also, enabling you to find the best talent.

This is our side project, and just starts. We encourage you to publish your jobs (valid for 30 days) on CooZilla.com, although there are no enough information to offer now.

We focus on Web programming, designing, and system administration.
